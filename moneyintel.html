Money Intel Mobile and Email Redesign

  • Team:Anuja, Chijioke, Sonia, David
  • Client:Money Intelligence
  • Timeline:2.5 weeks
Project Brief

Our Team had diverse backgrounds: A lawyer, A teacher, A financial analyst, and a project manager

401(K) provider, making investment easier for individuals and cheaper for management. Client goals were to design for mobile, improve clarity of the dashboard, and increase, signups, rollovers , and contributions.



 
User interviews and testing

After meeting with the client, we set up sessions with current users and folks who fit our target audience.



Current users typically had a high degree of financial knowledge, and were found to be likely to consolidate their investments in another location.



We found an investment opportunity with users who were less financially savvy, and decided to design for them.

An informed design process.
